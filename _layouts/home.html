About The CodeCamp

With joint efforts of Club of Technical Students (CoTS) and Innovative Computer Engineering Students’ Society (iCES), a themed CodeCamp is taking place from July 18-20 in the premises of Paschimanchal Campus, Lamachaur, Pokhara. The theme selected for this year is ‘Human Aid’ referring to any human assistance through technology – be it software, hardware or a subtle combination of both. This CodeCamp is organized on the occasion of a technical festival Vector-Direct Your Vision whose major objective is to brainstorm ideas, build and innovate systems and apply them to solve real-life problems.
