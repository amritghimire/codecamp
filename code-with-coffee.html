About The Code With Coffee Season 2

An open coding competition titled ‘Code With Coffee.’ was organized by Innovative computer Engineering Students' Society (i-CES) on 29th Asar of 2074. As a continuity to the competition, we are organizing Code with Coffee Season 2 in joint collaboration of i-CES and COTS on Ashad 25th 2075.

Code with coffee is 1 day event going to be organized in Paschimanchal Campus on Ashad 25th as a pre-event to Technical Festival going to be organized in Paschimanchal Campus on Shrawan 3,4 and 5th and CodeCamp 2018 going to be organized on 18-20 July. It is a team wise Coding competition with 2 or 3 team members.Winner team of this competition will get an wildcard free entry to CodeCamp 2018, an Internship if interested along with the Prize. So We strongly suggest everyone to participate in the program.

CodeCamp 2018


Club of Technical Students (CoTS) and Innovative Computer Engineering Students’ Society (iCES) is organizing a CodeCamp with the theme "Human Aid" on the occasion of technical festival held in Paschimanchal Campus on the pre-scheduled date. This event will make use of Information Technology to build and innovative applicable systems with the brainstorming of ideas to solve real-world problems.
